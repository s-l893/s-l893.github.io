About Me

I am currently a second year Mechatronics Engineering student at The University of Western Ontario (UWO) in London, Ontario. I joined the General Engineering program at Western in September 2024, which further helped me discover my passion for Mechatronics Engineering, which gave me discipline to excel in my studies to pursue this engineering path for the remainder of my undergraduate studies. From a young age, I knew I was destined to become an engineer. I have always been passionate about technology and learning how various devices and contraptions work while challenging myself to think about how I could improve upon their existing design. I am familiar with a variety of tools, ranging from CAD to coding languages and frameworks to physical microcontrollers and PCB design tools. Beyond engineering, I am an avid car enthusiast with a longstanding fascination for automotive technology. Since childhood, I have been captivated by the cutting-edge innovations that automakers integrate into their vehicles, and I actively engage with this passion through driving and hands-on maintenance work as a hobby. In my personal life, I value the strong relationships I have cultivated with a close-knit group of friends, many of whom I have known since my youth. I deeply value loyalty and cherish the time spent with those closest to me.
